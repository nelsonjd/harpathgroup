What we do

The Harpath Group curates independent hotels at great prices, to save you time in your search for a wonderful stay in Italy.

Independent-focused

All the hotels on our site are independently owned and many are family-run. There are no chain hotels listed on the Harpath Group.

Goodbye short-term rental industry

A stay at a hotel, in place of a short-term rental, can avoid negative disruptions to local residents' rental market. In many cases a hotel might be the cheaper to boot.
